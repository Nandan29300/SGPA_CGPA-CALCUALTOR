Hello! I am Nandan P Aghera, the creator of the VCET GPA & CGPA Calculator.

Who Am I?

I am a student pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from the batch of 2021-2025 at Vivekananda College of Engineering & Technology, PUTTUR. With a passion for simplifying the academic journey and keep track of it.

Why I Built This Website?

I understand the challenges that students face when it comes to calculating their GPA and CGPA. Many online tools are available, but they often require entering subject credit details and total semester credits, which can be time-consuming and cumbersome.

To overcome this issue, i decided to build our website. My goal was to create a user-friendly tool that enables students to calculate their GPA and CGPA without the hassle of entering credit details.

What Makes this Different?

  • Preloaded Courses: My website currently features courses available at VCET, puttur. I have eliminated the need for students to manually enter course details.
  • Ease of Use: With just a few clicks, students can select their regulation, course, semester, and grades to calculate their GPA and CGPA.

My Vision

While I have started with courses available in our VCET campus, i have plans to expand my offerings. In the future, i aim to include different courses available in VCET campus, making it even more convenient for students across the university.

Thank you for choosing the VCET GPA & CGPA Calculator. I hope my tool simplifies your academic journey and helps you achieve your goals.
